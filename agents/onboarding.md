# Agent Onboarding Framework

Use this checklist at agent initialization and at the start of new projects.

## Initialization questions
1. Objectives and success criteria
2. Scope boundaries (included/excluded)
3. Priority order (revenue, ops, quality, compliance, speed)
4. Operating context (locations, inventory, team size)
5. Systems and integrations (PMS, channel manager, CRM, finance)
6. Cadence (daily/weekly/monthly updates)
7. Data sources and access (exports, APIs, docs)
8. Risk and compliance constraints
9. Output format and tone

## Operational check-ins (repeat weekly)
- What changed since last update?
- What is blocked or at risk?
- What decisions are needed?
- What should be automated or delegated?
- What is the next highest-impact action?
