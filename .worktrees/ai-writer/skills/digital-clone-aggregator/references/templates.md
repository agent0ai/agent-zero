# Templates

## Daily digest (Telegram)

Headline: Daily Architecture + Tech + Business Digest (YYYY-MM-DD)

- Top 3 architecture signals
  - Title - 1-2 lines of impact + link
- Top 3 tech signals
  - Title - 1-2 lines of impact + link
- Top 3 business signals
  - Title - 1-2 lines of impact + link
- Action prompts
  - What to investigate next
  - Decisions to revisit

## Weekly brief

Headline: Weekly Architecture Review (Week of YYYY-MM-DD)

- Trend summary (5 bullets)
- Key decisions to consider (3 bullets)
- Risks and mitigations (3 bullets)
- Opportunities (3 bullets)
- Suggested experiments (3 bullets)

## Architecture review output (ADR style)

- Context
- Options (2-3) with tradeoffs
- Decision and rationale
- Follow-up actions
- Open questions
